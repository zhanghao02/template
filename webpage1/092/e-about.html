Shanghai Jiao Tong University School

Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of information security engineering, founded in October 2000, by the national Ministry of education, Ministry of science and technology, Shanghai municipal government of national information security professionals in the first college to build a training base for training senior information security professionals including undergraduate, graduate, doctoral and graduate students of different levels, including "communication and information system", "computer application technology" two major research direction. Information security college always open joint education guiding ideology, to develop in the international academic community has a certain status, professional technical and management talents of information security have influence in the domestic industry, the formation of the internationally renowned information security applications based research high school goal. The college has formed a unique training mode of discipline construction and personnel, and establish a strategic partnership with many famous universities and enterprises at home and abroad. School of information security engineering as the 863 national information security personnel training base, to speed up the upgrading of the core competitiveness of China's information security technology, to promote the process of China's information security industry, provides a strong guarantee for the training of information security professionals are urgently needed by the state.

Professor, associate professor of the school has more than 20 people, has hired more than 20 well-known professional in the field of information security as a part-time professor at the school, the formation of a team with more than 50 young teachers and more than 200 doctoral and master of research team. The college has undertaken more than 309 scientific research projects and national key project, including the demonstration project, the national information security application of national science and technology projects, the national 973 project, the National 863 major projects, the National Natural Science Fund project, the national defense basic research and military mission type, made a series of important achievements in scientific research, two etc. National Science and technology progress award 1, provincial science and technology progress prize 4, two prize 5, third-prize 1, has authorized more than 80 patents, and participate in a series of important national information security standards and regulations formulated construction.

Including the important scientific research base of information security disciplines: National Engineering Laboratory information content analysis; network information security research center of the Ministry of education management and service engineering; Shanghai city comprehensive management information security technology research laboratory; Shanghai Jiao Tong University information security technical service laboratory; Joint Research Laboratory of Paris Telecom and the Shanghai Jiao Tong University security and trust.
